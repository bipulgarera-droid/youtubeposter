# Thumbnail & Title Style Guide

> This directive documents the visual and text patterns for generating YouTube thumbnails.
> Reference channel style: Economic/geopolitical explainers (Chill Financial Historian style).

---

## Title-to-Thumbnail Text Correlation

The thumbnail text is a **2-4 word emotional distillation** of the title:

| Video Title | Thumbnail Text |
|-------------|----------------|
| Germany's $500 Billion Mistake (The Green Energy Trap) | IT FAILED |
| Why Invading the United States is IMPOSSIBLE (It's Not the Army) | ZERO CHANCE |
| Why France is POORER Than You Think (The Economic Truth) | FRANCE IS BROKE |
| Why Europe's Economy is Collapsing (The 6 Fatal Wounds) | EUROPE IS DYING |
| The Slow DEATH of The Petrodollar (And What Comes Next) | IT'S OVER |
| The Venezuela Economy That Nailed Maduro (The Verdict) | THE REAL REASON |
| Why Italy Can't Grow (The Curse of The Lira) | WHY ITALY IS DYING |
| Why the US Wants to BUY Greenland (It's Not a Joke) | IT'S NOT A JOKE |
| How Housing SWALLOWED Canada's Economy (The Real Estate Trap) | IT'S A PONZI? |
| How Norway Got Rich (And Why They Don't Spend It) | THE NORWAY CHEAT CODE |
| How America Became a Superpower (The Real Reason) | IT WASN'T FREEDOM |
| The End of Economic Growth (The Population Collapse) | GROWTH IS DEAD |
| How to Bankrupt a Country in 5 Easy Steps (The Venezuela Story) | HOW TO DELETE A COUNTRY |
| Why German Engineers Are Obsolete (The Hidden Crisis) | IT'S OVER |
| Why The French Economy Is UNREFORMABLE (The Union Trap) | FRANCE IS UNFIXABLE |

### Pattern Rules for Thumbnail Text:
1. Extract the emotional core of the title
2. Use 2-4 words MAX
3. Types of text:
   - **Doom phrase**: "IT FAILED", "IT'S OVER", "[X] IS DYING"
   - **Question**: "IT'S A PONZI?" (use ? sparingly)
   - **Teaser**: "THE REAL REASON", "THE CHEAT CODE"
   - **Contrarian reveal**: "IT WASN'T FREEDOM"
   - **Direct from title**: Often matches the parenthetical phrase

## Title Formula

```
"Why [Country/Topic] is [NEGATIVE STATE] (The [Hook/Mystery])"
```

OR

```
"The [Dramatic Event] of [Subject] (And [Consequence])"
```

### Examples:
- "Why **France** is **POORER** Than You Think (**The Economic Truth**)"
- "The Slow **DEATH** of The **Petrodollar** (**And What Comes Next**)"

### Key Words to Include:
- DEATH, DYING, COLLAPSE, FAILED, IMPOSSIBLE, BROKE, TRAPPED, CRISIS
- Use ONE power word in ALL CAPS in title

## Thumbnail Visual Composition

### Layout (Left-to-Right):
```
[CHARACTER]    [CHAOS/SUBJECT]    [BIG TEXT]
  (left 30%)     (center 40%)      (right 30%)
```

### Character (Left Side):
- **Style**: Semi-realistic cartoon/illustration (NOT photorealistic)
- **Position**: Usually left 1/3, but can vary
- **Orientation**: 
  - Facing viewer with shocked expression (most common)
  - OR back to viewer, looking at the chaos (contemplative mood)

**Character Types by Topic:**
| Topic | Character |
|-------|-----------|
| Generic economic | Worried businessman in suit |
| Italy/Old Europe | Elderly man with cane, sad expression |
| USA | Uncle Sam figure, or man in suit |
| Norway/Viking | Bearded Viking-style man |
| Canada/Housing | Casual dressed young person |
| France | Stylish man in suit, red tie |
| Germany | Engineer/worker turning away |
| Military/US | Soldier silhouette |

**Character Emotions:**
- Shocked (hands on head)
- Worried (biting nails)
- Pointing at chaos
- Holding something (money, magnifying glass)
- Back turned (contemplating doom)

### Central Subject (Middle):
- **Iconic landmarks**: Eiffel Tower, Brandenburg Gate, Capitol Building, Colosseum
- **Financial symbols**: Dollar sign (often on FIRE), Euro coin/gear, oil barrels, gold bars
- **Destruction elements**: Fire, smoke, debris, cracked buildings, floods
- **Props with text**: "CLOSED", "SOLD OVER ASKING", "DEBT", "MADE IN [X]"
- **Metaphorical objects**: Hourglass (time running out), padlock (trapped), chains

### Text (Right Side):
- **Position**: Right 1/3, stacked vertically
- **Font**: Heavy/bold sans-serif
- **Color**: WHITE with BLACK stroke/outline
- **Size**: LARGE - each word on its own line
- **Style**: ALL CAPS, no lowercase

```

CENTER: [Subject with iconic elements]

STYLE:
- Semi-realistic cartoon illustration
```

## Title Formula Variations

### Formula 1: Why [X] is [NEGATIVE] (The [Hook])
```
"Why [Country] is [NEGATIVE STATE] (The [Mystery/Hook])"
```
Examples:
- Why France is POORER Than You Think (The Economic Truth)
- Why German Engineers Are Obsolete (The Hidden Crisis)
- Why The French Economy Is UNREFORMABLE (The Union Trap)

### Formula 2: How [X] [DRAMATIC VERB] (And [Consequence])
```
"How [Subject] [Dramatic Verb] (And [What Happens Next])"
```
Examples:
- How Norway Got Rich (And Why They Don't Spend It)
- How Housing SWALLOWED Canada's Economy (The Real Estate Trap)
- How America Became a Superpower (The Real Reason)

### Formula 3: The [DRAMATIC EVENT] of [Subject]
```
"The [Dramatic Noun] of [Subject] (And/The [Extra Hook])"
```
Examples:
- The Slow DEATH of The Petrodollar (And What Comes Next)
- The End of Economic Growth (The Population Collapse)
- The Venezuela Economy That Nailed Maduro (The Verdict)

### Formula 4: How to [PROVOCATIVE ACTION] ([The Story])
```
"How to [Provocative Action] ([The Context])"
```
Examples:
- How to Bankrupt a Country in 5 Easy Steps (The Venezuela Story)

## Implementation Notes

When generating thumbnails:
1. First generate the TITLE using one of the formulas above
2. Extract THUMBNAIL TEXT (2-4 words) - often from parenthetical or emotional distillation
3. Generate image with the visual composition (character + subject + text)
4. Overlay text using PIL (white + black stroke)
5. Ensure 16:9 aspect ratio, dark moody colors

Reference images stored in: `.tmp/thumbnails with titles refs/`
