## Title Formulas

### Formula 1: Why [X] is [NEGATIVE STATE] (The [Hook])
**Best for:** Country economic problems, expos√©s

```
Why [Country/Subject] is [NEGATIVE STATE] (The [Mystery/Hook])
```

**Components:**
- **[Country/Subject]**: Germany, France, Europe, etc.
- **[NEGATIVE STATE]**: POORER, DYING, COLLAPSING, OBSOLETE, UNREFORMABLE, IMPOSSIBLE
- **[Hook]**: The Economic Truth, The Hidden Crisis, The Union Trap

**Examples:**
- Why France is POORER Than You Think (The Economic Truth)
- Why German Engineers Are Obsolete (The Hidden Crisis)
- Why The French Economy Is UNREFORMABLE (The Union Trap)
- Why Europe's Economy is Collapsing (The 6 Fatal Wounds)
- Why Italy Can't Grow (The Curse of The Lira)

---

### Formula 2: How [X] [DRAMATIC VERB] (And/The [Consequence])
**Best for:** Origin stories, process explanations

```
How [Subject] [Dramatic Past Verb] (And [Consequence] / The [Explanation])
```

**Components:**
- **[Subject]**: Country name, Housing, America
- **[Dramatic Verb]**: SWALLOWED, Got Rich, Became a Superpower
- **[Consequence]**: And Why They Don't Spend It, And What Comes Next

**Examples:**
- How Norway Got Rich (And Why They Don't Spend It)
- How Housing SWALLOWED Canada's Economy (The Real Estate Trap)
- How America Became a Superpower (The Real Reason)

---

### Formula 3: The [DRAMATIC NOUN] of [Subject]
**Best for:** Finality, end of an era stories

```
The [Dramatic Noun/Event] of [Subject] (The [Hook])
```

**Components:**
- **[Dramatic Noun]**: DEATH, End, Collapse, Verdict
- **[Subject]**: The Petrodollar, Economic Growth

**Examples:**
- The Slow DEATH of The Petrodollar (And What Comes Next)
- The End of Economic Growth (The Population Collapse)
- The Venezuela Economy That Nailed Maduro (The Verdict)

---

### Formula 4: [Subject]'s $[X] Billion/Trillion [Disaster]
**Best for:** Quantified failure stories

```
[Country/Company]'s $[Number] [Billion/Trillion] [Failure Word] (The [Hook])
```

**Examples:**
- Germany's $500 Billion Mistake (The Green Energy Trap)

---

### Formula 5: How to [PROVOCATIVE ACTION] ([Context])
**Best for:** Satirical/provocative takes

```
How to [Provocative Action] ([The Context Story])
```

**Examples:**
- How to Bankrupt a Country in 5 Easy Steps (The Venezuela Story)

## Power Words to Include

### MUST have ONE of these in ALL CAPS in title:
- DEATH / DYING
- COLLAPSE / COLLAPSING
- FAILED / FAILURE
- IMPOSSIBLE
- POORER / BROKE / BANKRUPT
- TRAPPED / TRAP
- CRISIS
- SWALLOWED
- UNREFORMABLE
- OBSOLETE

### Parenthetical hooks:
- The [X] Truth
- The [X] Crisis
- The [X] Trap
- The [X] Story
- The Real Reason
- It's Not [obvious thing]
- And What Comes Next
- And Why They [unexpected action]
- The [Number] Fatal [Wounds/Steps/Reasons]

---

## Title Structure Rules

1. **Length**: 50-70 characters ideal
2. **One POWER WORD in caps**: DEATH, IMPOSSIBLE, POORER, etc.
3. **Parenthetical hook**: Always end with (The X) or (And Y)
4. **Country/Subject first**: Lead with the entity being discussed
5. **Question framing**: Use "Why" for problems, "How" for processes
6. **Contrast/Surprise**: "POORER Than You Think", "It's Not the Army"

## Title-to-Thumbnail Text Mapping

Once title is decided, extract thumbnail text:

| Title Pattern | Thumbnail Text Strategy |
|---------------|------------------------|
| Why [X] is [STATE] | "[X] IS [STATE]" or "IT'S OVER" |
| How [X] [Verb] (The [Hook]) | Use [Hook] or reaction: "THE REAL REASON" |
| [X]'s $N Billion Mistake | "IT FAILED" |
| The DEATH of [X] | "IT'S OVER" or "[X] IS DEAD" |
| Parenthetical is question | Use question: "IT'S A PONZI?" |
| Parenthetical is "Not [X]" | Use directly: "IT'S NOT A JOKE" |

## Implementation Notes

When generating a title:
1. Identify the topic/country
2. Identify the angle (failure, origin, collapse, etc.)
3. Select appropriate formula
4. Insert power word in CAPS
5. Add parenthetical hook
6. Extract thumbnail text
